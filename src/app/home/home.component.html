Dartmoor Tree Work

Heavily decayed Beech trees removed on Dartmoor. We were called in to dismantle these Beech trees due to heavy decay at the bottom & the risk they posed to all visitors to the Christmas tree farm & the crop below. With a little bit of rigging we managed to avoid any damage to the young Christmas trees below, all timber was cut into manageable lengths and left on site & the wood chip was fired into the Christmas trees to act as a weed suppressant.
